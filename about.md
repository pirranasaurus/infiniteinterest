# About

I’m an optimistic, happy bloke that loves tech. As the site title suggest, I have a continuous hunger to learn and enjoy the process.
What I am less good at is documenting that journey. Every tech nerd needs their own website, right?! So here we are, the push to make sure I don't forget to list the highs and lows along the way.
You can expect to see posts on experiments, stuff I've been building, tools used. I'll also be making comment on interesting news, especially in the cyber security world.

I planning on adding more stuff to the home lab set up that has evolved over time, and obviously have to mention the elephant in the room, AI. I'll explore how I'm using it, what gaps it is filling and the different use cases from home/work.

If something here helps you play and learn too, that’s a win.
